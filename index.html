Who We Are

We are a group of PhD and Masters graduate students at University at Buffalo.

Everyone today is doing some or the other form of applied artificial intelligence, machine learning, or data science and others are even doing dedicated research efforts towards it.

But there are a lot of different people working in different silos.

Some who are really great at what they do having spent years meticulously towards understanding and moving forward, others who are just starting.

The vision for Transcend is to become the epicentre for all these people.
